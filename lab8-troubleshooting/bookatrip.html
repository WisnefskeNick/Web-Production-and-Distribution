Book a trip

Our travel packages are not your run-of-the-mill vacations. We take care of everything — and we do mean everything. When you book a trip through Expeditions Adventure Travel, you will stay in a four or five star hotel, all of your meals are covered. In addition, your price also includes all of the extras, such as rental cars, bike or ski rentals, admissions fees for zip lining or similar activities.

We take all the headache out of travel planning. Just let us know when and where you'd like to go and we'll take care of the rest. Take a look at some of our existing packages below, or contact us to create a unique trip just for you.

Easter Island

This exotic trip includes tours of the famous Moai, the monolithic figures carved by the Rapa Nui between 1200 - 1500 A.D., as well as scuba diving adventures.

Travel Dates Prices*
October - February $7,500/per person
March - June $6,500/per person
July - September $5,000/per person

Sail Italy

Sail the Mediterranean. You'll leave from the scenic port of Naples, where you will travel to the islands of Sardinia and Sicily. While on Sicily you will visit Mount Etna, the tallest active volcano on the European continent.

Travel Dates Prices*
June - September $6,500/per person
October - May $5,500/per person

Colorado Mountain Biking

A trip doesn't have to be to a foreign destination to be adventurous. Book one of our many mountain biking trips available in Colorado. Go from Telluride to Durango to experience some of the most rugged and beautiful peaks the Rockies have to offer.

Travel Dates Prices*
June - October $4,000/per person
May $2,500/per person
